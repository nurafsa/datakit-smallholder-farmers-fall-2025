East Africa Weather Patterns

     January - March: Hottest period, generally dry.
     March - May (Long Rains): Heavy rainfall, sometimes making travel difficult, but lush landscapes.
     June - September (Cool/Dry): Coolest, driest months, popular for safaris due to clear skies and concentrated wildlife.
     October - December (Short Rains): Brief rains, often good for wildlife before the main dry season
